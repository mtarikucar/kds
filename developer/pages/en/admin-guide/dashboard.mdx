# Dashboard

**Menu path:** Business → Dashboard &nbsp;•&nbsp; **URL:** `/dashboard`
**Access:** All roles (content varies by role)  •  **Plan:** All plans

## Purpose

The Dashboard is the **landing screen** you see after signing in. It brings three things together at a glance: a fast jump to the **primary action** for your role, **shortcuts** to the most-used screens, and your plan's **usage meters**. The goal is to make it clear where to start your day and to keep how close you are to your plan limits visible.

## What's on the screen

- **Primary action card (hero):** The main job highlighted for your role. For cashier/waiter roles this is usually **POS**; for the kitchen role it's the **Kitchen Display (KDS)**.
- **Quick shortcuts:** One-tap access to frequently used screens such as Tables, Users, Customers, QR Codes, Reports and Subscription/Settings.
- **Usage meters (quota):** "used / limit" indicators for **Users**, **Branches**, **Products** and **Monthly orders**. These values come from your plan's limits.

<Callout type="info">
  The usage meters are read against your plan's **limits**. As you approach a limit (e.g.
  monthly orders or product count) this is where you'll notice it's time to upgrade — see
  [Plans](/en/plans) and [Choosing & upgrading](/en/plans/choosing-and-upgrading).
</Callout>

<Callout type="warning">
  The dashboard looks **different** by role: restricted roles such as KITCHEN/COURIER only
  see their relevant shortcuts and primary action. If a card doesn't appear, that screen
  isn't in your plan or role — see [Users & Roles](/en/admin-guide/users).
</Callout>
