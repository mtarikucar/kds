# Integrations

Connect HummyTummy to the external services you use. These integrations are set up from the **Settings** screen and tie your order, invoice and customer flows to the outside world.

<Callout type="info">
  Integration settings are configured by the **ADMIN** role. Most integrations are
  tied to a **plan feature** or a **marketplace add-on** — if it isn't in your plan,
  the corresponding tab doesn't appear. See [Plans](https://help.hummytummy.com/en/plans) and
  [Marketplace](https://help.hummytummy.com/en/marketplace).
</Callout>
