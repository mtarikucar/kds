# Choosing & upgrading

Plan selection and changes run through two screens in the panel:

- **Plan selection screen** — lists the sellable plans and lets you pick monthly or yearly.
- **Change plan screen** — upgrades or downgrades a live subscription.

All prices are **TRY** and **VAT-inclusive**; the amount you see on screen is the amount you'll be charged. To work with plans and payments you need the **Admin (ADMIN)** or **Manager (MANAGER)** role in your business; the authority to actually change a plan is **Admin (ADMIN) only**.

## Seeing the sellable plans

The plan-selection screen shows only the plans that are **on sale**: Basic, Pro, and Business. The Trial plan you received at sign-up and the retired old Free plan do not appear in this list.

Each plan card shows the monthly and yearly price (₺, VAT-inclusive), the features included in that plan, and its usage limits. When a campaign is active, the discounted price is shown right on the card — and the discounted price you see is exactly the amount that will appear on the payment screen.

## Selection flow: which screen do you land on?

When you press **Select** on a plan card, the system looks at your business's current subscription status and routes you to the right screen:

<Steps>

### You have a live subscription and picked a different plan
You go to the **Change plan** screen. Upgrading or downgrading only makes sense within a live (active or trial) subscription period.

### You have a live subscription and picked the plan you're already on
Nothing happens; you can't switch to the same plan.

### Your subscription has expired or been cancelled, or you have none
You go to a fresh **checkout** screen and start a new, full-period subscription.

### You still have trial time left
If your trial is still running, the payment step is skipped and the trial flow takes over; you continue without being charged.

</Steps>

## Upgrade (immediate, prorated charge)

Moving to a higher plan while on a live subscription counts as an **upgrade**. When you pick a more expensive plan on the Change plan screen, the system calculates a **prorated amount based on the days remaining** in the period and shows it to you — so you only pay the difference for the remaining time, not a whole new month or year.

<Steps>

### Pick the new (higher) plan
On the Change plan screen, choose the target plan and the monthly/yearly cycle.

### Confirm the prorated amount
The screen shows the prorated difference to pay (₺, VAT-inclusive). The fewer days remaining, the smaller the difference.

### Complete the payment
Payment is taken via PayTR (card) or bank transfer. The upgrade is applied **after** the payment is confirmed, and the new features/limits open up right away.

</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  The prorated amount is based on the number of days left until your billing period ends. If there's a difference to pay for the remaining time, the upgrade only takes effect once that payment is collected.
</Callout>

## Downgrade (applied at period end)

Moving to a lower (cheaper) plan counts as a **downgrade** and is **not applied immediately** — it's **scheduled for the end** of your current billing period. That way you keep using the higher plan for the period you've already paid for.

- Before downgrading, the system checks whether your current usage fits the target plan's limits. For example, if you have **4 branches**, you can't downgrade to the Basic plan's single-branch limit; you'd first need to bring your usage under the limits.
- When a downgrade is scheduled, the screen shows you the **period-end date** on which the change will take effect.

<Callout type="warning">
  If you try to make a new change while one is already scheduled, the system asks you to cancel the existing scheduled change first. If you cancel a downgrade before the period ends, you simply stay on your current plan.
</Callout>

## Payment methods: PayTR and bank transfer

You can complete a new subscription or an upgrade payment with one of two methods:

- **Card (PayTR)** — the default. PayTR charges in **TRY** only. You enter your card details on the payment screen; once the payment is confirmed your subscription activates automatically.
- **Bank transfer (havale/EFT)** — an alternative method. The screen shows you the platform's bank/IBAN details; after you make the transfer, your payment is activated **by staff confirmation**. This option only appears if an IBAN has been configured on the platform side.

<Callout type="info">
  Add-ons you buy from the Marketplace use the **same card payment flow** and only activate once payment is complete. See [Marketplace](/en/marketplace).
</Callout>

## Acquiring features later

When you need a feature later, there are two paths:

1. **Plan upgrade** — move to a higher tier to unlock the full feature set and higher limits (the upgrade flow on this page).
2. **Marketplace add-on** — add a single capability without changing plans. See [Marketplace](/en/marketplace).
