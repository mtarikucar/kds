# Trial & billing

## 7-day trial

Every new business is automatically placed on the **Trial** plan at sign-up:

- **Duration:** 7 days.
- **Scope:** **all of the Business plan's features** and **unlimited** limits. So during the trial you can test the product at full capacity without hitting any feature restriction.
- **Not sellable:** the Trial plan is not listed on the plan-selection screen; it isn't a plan you buy — it's assigned to you at sign-up.

The trial runs as a separate onboarding plan and isn't tied to any paid plan. You're expected to choose a paid plan before your trial period ends.

## What happens when the trial ends (the lock)

If your trial period elapses **without you choosing a paid plan**, your business is locked. This is not a silent downgrade to a lower plan — it's an **explicit lock**: you have to choose a plan to continue.

<Steps>

### The app locks
When the trial ends and you try to navigate the panel, you'll see the message "Your trial has ended. Choose a plan to continue." and access to the normal screens stops.

### What you can still do
Even while locked you can reach the **plan-selection and payment screens**: you can view the plans, pick one, and pay. The other working screens (POS, kitchen, reports, etc.) stay closed until you choose a plan.

### Automatic redirect
While locked, the app automatically sends you to the plan-selection screen, where choosing a plan unlocks everything.

</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
  The same lock applies not only when the trial ends, but also when your subscription **expires** or is **cancelled**. A subscription that is past due (PAST_DUE) is still treated as live during its 7-day grace period and isn't locked.
</Callout>

## Billing cycle

You choose one of two billing cycles: **Monthly** or **Yearly**. The cycle you pick determines the period length and the amount you pay on subscription creation, upgrade, and downgrade. **Paying yearly is cheaper than 12× the monthly amount** (about 17% savings).

You can view and download your past invoices from the invoices screen in the panel; each invoice lists its date, amount, and period.

## VAT-inclusive pricing

All advertised plan prices are **gross** (VAT-inclusive) amounts, and you are charged exactly that amount — no extra VAT is added on top at invoice time. On the invoice the total is broken down so the VAT it contains is shown as a separate line.

**Example — Pro monthly ₺1,299:**

| Line | Amount |
| --- | --- |
| Subtotal (net) | ₺1,082.50 |
| VAT (20%) | ₺216.50 |
| **Total (charged)** | **₺1,299.00** |

<Callout type="info">
  Your business's tax ID is written onto the invoice **as it was at the time the invoice was issued**. Even if you change your tax ID later, your past invoices are unaffected.
</Callout>

## Cancellation and reactivation

- **Cancel** — you can cancel your subscription from the panel (Admin only). You can choose whether the cancellation takes effect **immediately or at period end**, and optionally enter a short **cancellation reason**.
- **Reactivate** — you can reactivate a cancelled subscription from the panel (Admin only).
