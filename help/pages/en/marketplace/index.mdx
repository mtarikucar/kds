# Marketplace

**Menu path:** Side menu → Marketplace &nbsp;•&nbsp; **URL:** `/admin/marketplace`
**Access:** ADMIN, MANAGER (purchase confirmation is ADMIN-only) • **Plan:** All plans

The Marketplace is the catalogue of add-ons you can buy **individually** on top
of your plan. A plan gives you a feature and limit baseline; add-ons **raise**
that baseline: an extra KDS screen, an extra branch, an e-Fatura integration,
advanced reports, and so on.

The Marketplace screen is open on every plan. Each card shows what the add-on
does, its price (₺), and whether it's recurring or a one-time service. When you
buy an add-on, the entitlement it grants (for example one extra KDS screen or a
new integration) is **added to your account automatically** — the related
limit/feature becomes usable in the panel right away.

<Callout type="info">
  All prices are in **TRY** and add-on prices are shown **KDV-inclusive**.
  Payments are collected through PayTR.
</Callout>
