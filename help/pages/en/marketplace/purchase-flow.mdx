# Purchase Flow

**Menu path:** Side menu → Marketplace &nbsp;•&nbsp; **URL:** `/admin/marketplace`
**Access:** ADMIN, MANAGER (payment confirmation and cancellation are ADMIN-only) • **Plan:** All plans

The only way to buy an add-on is to add it to the cart in the Marketplace and
**pay via PayTR**. No entitlement (limit or feature) is added to your account
until the payment completes; there is no way to activate an add-on for free.

<Callout type="info">
  Operator comps can only be issued by the HummyTummy team / superadmin. On your
  side, the only way to buy is the paid flow.
</Callout>

## End-to-end flow

<Steps>

### Pick an add-on

Find the add-on you want to buy on the Marketplace screen. Each card shows what
the add-on does, its price (₺), and whether it's recurring or a one-time service.

### Add to cart

Add the add-on to the cart. You can put several add-ons (and, if needed, plan or
hardware lines) in the same cart; the screen shows the total **KDV-inclusive**.
The total is always recalculated on the server, so the amount you pay is
trustworthy.

### Continue to payment

Use the **Pay / Continue** button to move to the payment step. The screen
redirects you to the **PayTR** secure payment page, where each item in your cart
appears as its own line (e.g. *"Extra KDS screen (monthly)"*).

### Complete the payment

Pay on the PayTR page. Once the payment is confirmed, you are returned to the app.

### Entitlements activate

The moment the payment is confirmed, the entitlement the add-on grants is **added
to your account automatically**. For example, if you bought one extra KDS screen,
your KDS-screen limit goes up by one in the panel right away; if you bought an
integration, the related setting/feature is enabled.

</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
  If you reach the payment page but abandon the payment, you are not charged and
  no entitlement is added. The purchase only completes once the payment is
  confirmed.
</Callout>

## How entitlements stack

Add-ons stack on top of your plan; multiple sources of the same kind combine like
this:

- **Limits** (KDS screens, stations, tablets, branches) → **summed.** For
  example, if your plan gives 2 KDS screens and you buy 1 "Extra KDS screen",
  the total becomes **3**.
- **Features** (advanced reports, priority support, multi-location, etc.) →
  **enabled** if any source enables it.
- **Integrations** (e-Fatura, Yemeksepeti, Getir, Trendyol) → all accumulate
  side by side; you can use several delivery integrations at once.

Buying the same capacity add-on twice to double a limit is blocked: if you
already own an add-on, trying to buy the same item a second time is rejected and
your card is not charged again.

## Dependencies

Some add-ons require something else to already be in place. Today the only add-on
like this in the catalogue is **Hugin yazarkasa**: it requires the **PRO plan**.
If you are not on PRO, the purchase is blocked and you're told to upgrade your
plan first.

## Cancellation and period-end behaviour

Only an **ADMIN** can cancel an add-on (it's a billing decision). You have two
options:

- **Cancel at period end (default):** the add-on **stays active until the end of
  the period**, and the entitlements it grants keep working until that date. When
  the period ends it closes automatically and the related limit/feature is
  revoked.
- **Cancel immediately:** the add-on closes at once and the entitlements it
  grants are **revoked right away**.

<Callout type="info">
  Recurring add-ons run on a 30-day period window; your real billing cycle aligns
  with your main subscription's cycle. One-time service lines (e.g. on-site setup)
  don't recur and have no period to cancel.
</Callout>
