# HummyTummy Help Center

**HummyTummy** is a cloud-based restaurant management system: QR menu, POS, kitchen display (KDS), order & table management, inventory, reports and multi-branch — in one panel.

This portal is for **restaurant owners and their teams**: it walks through how to set the system up, how to use each screen day to day, and how to manage your plan.
