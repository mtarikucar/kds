# Invoices

**Menu path:** Back Office → Invoices &nbsp;•&nbsp; **URL:** `/admin/invoices`
**Access:** ADMIN, MANAGER  •  **Plan:** All plans (issuing an e-Invoice requires a fiscal integration)

## Purpose

The Invoices screen lists every **sales invoice** you've issued in one place. For each invoice you can see its number, customer, date, amount and tax; you also track its status and its **sync status** with the e-Invoice integration. From here you can **resend** an invoice that hasn't been delivered to the integration yet, or **cancel** an invoice.

<Callout type="info">
  This screen **lists and manages** invoices. The official issuance of an invoice as a
  real e-Invoice/e-Archive document happens through the fiscal provider connection
  configured under **Integrations → e-Invoice**. Without a connection, records still
  appear in the list but their sync status stays **not sent**.
</Callout>

## How to read the list

The table shows the following columns for each invoice:

- **Invoice No** — the invoice number.
- **Customer** — the name the invoice was issued to (`-` if empty).
- **Date** — the issue date (`dd.mm.yyyy`).
- **Amount** and **Tax** — formatted in the invoice's currency (₺/TRY by default).
- **Status** — the invoice lifecycle: **Draft (DRAFT)**, **Issued (ISSUED)**, **Sent (SENT)** or **Cancelled (CANCELLED)**.
- **Sync status** — the result of sending to the integration: **Synced**, **Failed** or **not sent**.

<Callout type="info">
  If you hover over the label of an invoice whose sync failed, the **error message**
  returned by the provider appears as a tooltip — use it to fix the problem and resend.
</Callout>

## Search and filtering

<Steps>
### Search

Type an invoice number or customer name into the search box at the top to narrow the list. As you type, the list jumps back to the first page.

### Status filter

Use the dropdown next to it to pick **Draft / Issued / Sent / Cancelled** and see only invoices in that status; "All statuses" shows everything.

### Pagination

The list is paginated 20 at a time. Use **Previous / Next** below the table to move between pages; a `page / total` indicator sits in the middle.
</Steps>

## Sync and cancel actions

The icons at the end of each row let you perform two actions:

- **Resend (sync)** — the refresh icon. It appears only on invoices that **haven't been synced yet** and **aren't cancelled**. It re-delivers the invoice to the e-Invoice integration.
- **Cancel** — the red cross icon. It appears on every invoice that **isn't already cancelled**; it moves the invoice into the cancelled status.

<Callout type="warning">
  Cancel and sync actions can have consequences that are hard to undo — especially
  after an invoice has been **sent** to the provider. Make sure you're on the correct
  invoice before confirming. Even though the plan and permission checks run in the UI,
  the final decision is made **on the server**: an unauthorized action or one that
  breaks the rules is rejected server-side.
</Callout>
