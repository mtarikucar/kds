## 1. Executive Summary

### Company Overview

HummyTummy is a cloud-native restaurant management platform that integrates Point of Sale (POS), Kitchen Display System (KDS), QR Menu ordering, Customer Relationship Management (CRM), and delivery platform connections into a single, unified solution.

### The Opportunity

The global restaurant technology market is experiencing rapid transformation driven by:
- Post-COVID digital acceleration
- Labor shortages requiring automation
- Customer expectations for seamless digital experiences
- Fragmentation of existing solutions creating integration headaches

### Our Solution

HummyTummy eliminates the complexity and cost of restaurant technology by providing:
- **Zero hardware investment** - Works on any existing device
- **1-hour setup** - No complex installation or training
- **Complete solution** - Replaces 4-5 separate vendors
- **Native delivery integration** - Connected to 5 major platforms

### Business Model

SaaS subscription model with four tiers:
- Free: $0/month (lead generation)
- Basic: $29.99/month (small restaurants)
- Pro: $79.99/month (full-service restaurants)
- Business: $199.99/month (chains, multi-location)

### Traction

- Product development complete (18+ months)
- 27+ backend modules implemented
- 5 delivery platform integrations built
- Multi-platform desktop app ready
- 5-language support

### Funding Request

**Seed Round: $250,000 - $500,000**

Use of funds:
- Product Development: 40%
- Sales & Marketing: 30%
- Operations: 20%
- Reserve: 10%

### 4.1 Core Products

#### Point of Sale (POS) System

**Features:**
- Multi-order type support (Dine-in, Takeaway, Delivery)
- Visual table management with drag-and-drop
- Product modifiers and variations
- Split payments and tip management
- Cash drawer tracking
- Receipt printing (thermal printers)
- Offline mode with sync

**Value Proposition:**
- Works on any device (tablet, phone, desktop)
- Intuitive interface requiring minimal training
- Real-time inventory updates

#### Kitchen Display System (KDS)

**Features:**
- Real-time WebSocket order updates
- Sound alerts for new orders
- Item-level status tracking (Pending → Preparing → Ready → Served)
- Preparation time analytics
- Multi-screen support for different stations
- Priority order sorting
- Delayed order alerts

**Value Proposition:**
- Eliminates paper tickets
- Reduces order errors by 80%
- Improves kitchen efficiency

#### QR Menu & Direct Ordering

**Features:**
- Table-specific QR code generation
- Mobile-optimized digital menu
- Direct customer ordering without waiter
- Product customization options
- Cart management
- Order status tracking
- Customizable branding (colors, logo, fonts)

**Value Proposition:**
- Reduces wait times by 40%
- Increases average order value by 15-20%
- No app download required

#### Customer Loyalty Program

**Features:**
- Automatic customer registration
- Tiered loyalty system:
  - Bronze (0-499 points): 1.0x multiplier
  - Silver (500-1999 points): 1.2x multiplier
  - Gold (2000-4999 points): 1.5x multiplier
  - Platinum (5000+ points): 2.0x multiplier
- Point earning (1 point per $1)
- Point redemption (100 points = $10)
- Welcome and birthday bonuses
- Referral system

**Value Proposition:**
- Increases repeat visits by 25%
- Builds customer database
- Enables targeted marketing

**Annual vs Monthly:**
- 17% discount for annual billing
- Increases customer lifetime value
- Improves cash flow predictability

### 5.2 Pricing Strategy

**Value-Based Pricing:**
- Free tier removes barrier to adoption
- Pricing based on value delivered, not costs
- Clear upgrade path as restaurants grow
- Competitive with alternatives

**Plan Differentiation:**

| Support | Community | Email | Priority | Dedicated |
